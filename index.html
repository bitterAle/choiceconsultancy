Who We Are

Choice Consultants is an indigenous owned and managed Kenyan firm of consulting engineers operating mainly in East Africa. The firm was formed and registerd on 20th August 2009 and concentrates mostly on offering consultancy services in building services. The main office is located at Tumaini house situated along north airport road.
